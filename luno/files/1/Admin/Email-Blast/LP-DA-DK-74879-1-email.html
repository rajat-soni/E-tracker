Discover decodable books to support your science of reading implementation


Phonic Books specializes in step-by-step, truly decodable books which supplement any structured literacy curriculum and ensure students receive the resources they need to practice their foundational reading skills and build a lifelong love of reading.

Teachers use Phonic Books for:
  • more decoding practice, supplementing their phonics instructional program in K-2,
  • small group instruction for those not reading at grade level in K-5, and
  • reading intervention in grade 3 and above.

Phonic Books take students from CVC word level text through to adjacent consonants, consonant digraphs, suffixes, and the introduction of alternative spellings for vowel sounds. The Dandelion series is for beginner readers in grades K-2, and our hi-lo catch-up readers are for older students in grades 3-5 who have gaps in their phonics knowledge.
